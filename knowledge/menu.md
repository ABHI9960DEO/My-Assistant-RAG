# Stellar Coffee Co. - Cafe Menu

This is the menu of prepared drinks and food available in our cafes. All
espresso drinks are pulled with our Aurora Blend by default; ask for Midnight
Reserve for a darker, bolder shot at no extra charge.

## Espresso drinks

- Espresso - $3.00 (double shot $3.75)
- Americano - $3.75
- Cappuccino - $4.50
- Caffe Latte - $5.00 (add a flavor for $0.75: vanilla, caramel, or hazelnut)
- Flat White - $5.00
- Mocha - $5.50

## Brewed and cold

- Drip coffee (Aurora Blend) - $3.25
- Pour-over (rotating single origin) - $5.00
- Comet Cold Brew (seasonal, May through September) - $5.00

## Not coffee

- Hot tea (black, green, or herbal) - $3.50
- Hot chocolate - $4.25
- Steamed milk ("babyccino") - $2.50

## Food

- Butter croissant - $3.75
- Almond croissant - $4.50
- Blueberry muffin - $3.50
- Banana bread (vegan) - $4.00

Milk options: whole, oat, almond, or soy. Oat and almond add $0.75. Prices
include tax. Cafe menu items are made fresh in store and are not shipped.
