# Stellar Coffee Co. - Company Handbook

Stellar Coffee Co. was founded in March 2019 by Maya Okonkwo in Portland, Oregon.
Our mission is to roast small-batch, single-origin coffee while paying farmers
above fair-trade prices.

## Locations

We operate three cafes:

- The original "Roastery" on Hawthorne Boulevard (opened 2019)
- A downtown kiosk inside the Pioneer Place mall (opened 2021)
- A drive-through on Sandy Boulevard (opened 2023)

## Loyalty program

Members of the "Stellar Circle" earn 1 star per dollar spent. Every 80 stars can
be redeemed for a free 12oz drink. Stars expire 12 months after they are earned.
Membership is free and can be started in the mobile app.

## Hours

All cafes open at 6:30 AM on weekdays and 7:00 AM on weekends. The Roastery
closes at 7:00 PM; the downtown kiosk and the drive-through both close at 5:00 PM.
