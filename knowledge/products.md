# Stellar Coffee Co. - Current Coffee Lineup

## Aurora Blend

A medium roast blending beans from Ethiopia and Colombia. Tasting notes:
blueberry, milk chocolate, and a soft citrus finish. This is our best seller
and the default coffee served in all cafes. $16 per 12oz bag.

## Midnight Reserve

A dark roast made from 100% Sumatran beans. Tasting notes: dark chocolate,
molasses, and cedar. Low acidity. Recommended for espresso. $18 per 12oz bag.

## Solstice Decaf

A Swiss-water-process decaf from Honduras. Tasting notes: caramel, almond, and
green apple. The caffeine is removed without chemicals. $17 per 12oz bag.

## Seasonal: Comet Cold Brew

Available only from May through September. Steeped for 18 hours and served over
ice. Made with the Aurora Blend. Sold as a ready-to-drink can for $5.
